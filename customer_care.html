Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We offer several shipping options to accommodate your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Next Day Delivery: Order before 2pm for next business day delivery

International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all full-price items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Final sale items are not eligible for return.

Returns are free for customers in the contiguous US. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

If you have any issues tracking your order, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country.

How do I care for my luxury items?

Each product comes with specific care instructions. In general:

  • Store leather goods in dust bags when not in use
  • Avoid exposure to direct sunlight and extreme temperatures
  • Use specialized cleaners for different materials
  • For fine jewelry, remove before swimming or applying cosmetics

SHIPPING & RETURNS

Home / Customer Care / Shipping & Returns

Shipping Information

Standard Shipping

We offer complimentary standard shipping on all orders over $500. For orders under $500, a flat rate of $15 applies.

  • Delivery within 3-5 business days
  • Order processing time: 1-2 business days
  • Tracking information provided

Express Shipping

For those who need their luxury items sooner, we offer express shipping options.

  • Delivery within 2-3 business days
  • Order processing time: 1 business day
  • Priority handling
  • $25 flat rate

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide with DHL International.

  • Delivery within 5-10 business days
  • Customs and import taxes may apply
  • Tracking and insurance included
  • Shipping rates vary by destination

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. You may return most unused items in their original condition within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

  • 30-day return window
  • Items must be unused and unworn
  • Original packaging and tags must be included
  • Proof of purchase required

Exchange Policy

If you need a different size or color, we offer free exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

  • Free return shipping for exchanges
  • Product must be in original condition
  • Exchanges subject to availability
  • We'll ship your new item once we receive the return

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, some items cannot be returned.

  • Earrings for pierced ears
  • Swimwear and intimate apparel
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items marked "Final Sale"

How to Return

Initiate Return

Log into your account and visit the Order History section. Select the item you wish to return and follow the prompts.

Alternatively, contact our customer care team who will guide you through the process.

Package Item

Place the item in its original packaging along with all tags and certificates. Include the original invoice or proof of purchase.

Use the prepaid shipping label provided or ship to our returns center address.

Ship & Track

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. Keep your tracking number for reference.

Once received, we'll process your return within 3-5 business days and notify you via email.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive your return, it takes 3-5 business days to process. After processing, your bank may take an additional 5-10 business days to post the refund to your account.
Who pays for return shipping?
We provide complimentary return shipping for all exchanges and for returns that are due to our error. For other returns, return shipping is the customer's responsibility unless you're a SCOTCH BLACK member.
What if my item arrives damaged?
We're sorry to hear that! Please contact our customer care team within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item and packaging. We will arrange for a return and expedite a replacement at no cost to you.
Can I return a gift?
Yes, gift items can be returned for store credit within 30 days of delivery. The return process is the same, and we'll issue a digital gift card to the original purchaser's email address.

Our customer care team is here to help

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I redeem my gift card?
You can redeem your gift card during checkout by entering the gift card code in the payment section. The amount will be deducted from your total order value.
Do gift cards expire?
Our luxury gift cards never expire. The recipient can use them at any time to purchase items from our collections.
Can I use multiple gift cards on one order?
Yes, you can use up to 3 gift cards per order. Simply enter each gift card code during the checkout process.
What if my purchase is less than the gift card amount?
The remaining balance will stay on the gift card and can be used for future purchases. You can check your gift card balance at any time in your account section.
Can I return items purchased with a gift card?
Yes, returns for items purchased with a gift card will be refunded to the original gift card. If the gift card is no longer available, we will issue a new one.

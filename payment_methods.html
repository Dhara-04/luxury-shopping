Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?
Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data, and we are PCI DSS compliant. We never store your complete credit card details on our servers.
What currencies do you accept?
We accept payments in USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, and JPY. The currency will be automatically selected based on your location, but you can change it at checkout.
Are there any additional fees for using certain payment methods?
No, we do not charge any additional fees for any payment method. The price you see at checkout is the final amount you will pay.
Can I save my payment details for future purchases?
Yes, you can securely save your payment details in your account for faster checkout. All saved information is encrypted and stored following strict security protocols.
What should I do if my payment is declined?
Payment declines can happen for various reasons. Please check that your card details are entered correctly, ensure you have sufficient funds, and verify that your card allows international transactions if applicable. If problems persist, please contact your bank or our customer service team.

Need assistance with payment?

Our customer care team is available to help with any payment-related questions

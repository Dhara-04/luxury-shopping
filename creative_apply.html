Creative Director Application

Lead our creative vision and shape the future of luxury fashion

Creative Director

Design & Innovation
Paris, France / New York, USA
Full-time
Posted 2 days ago

We are seeking a visionary Creative Director to lead our design team and shape the artistic direction of our brand. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in luxury fashion, an eye for detail, and the ability to translate cultural trends into commercially successful collections.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute the creative vision for seasonal collections
  • Lead and inspire a team of designers, stylists, and creatives
  • Collaborate with product development and merchandising teams
  • Oversee photoshoots, campaigns, and visual storytelling
  • Establish and maintain the brand's aesthetic across all touchpoints
  • Research and forecast fashion trends in the luxury market

Requirements

  • 10+ years of experience in creative leadership roles
  • Proven track record in luxury fashion or related industries
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating creative vision and execution
  • Excellent leadership and team management skills
  • Deep understanding of luxury market and consumer behavior
  • Bachelor's degree in Design, Fashion, or related field
  • Fluency in English; additional languages are a plus

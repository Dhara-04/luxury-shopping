We're seeking an innovative Digital Marketing Manager to lead our digital strategy and execution. You'll be responsible for developing and implementing comprehensive digital marketing campaigns that elevate our luxury brand and drive growth across all digital channels.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing strategies
  • Manage all digital marketing channels (SEO/SEM, email, social media, display advertising)

  • Manage digital marketing budget and ensure ROI targets are met

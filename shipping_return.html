Shipping & Returns

We want to ensure you have the best experience shopping with us. Learn about our shipping options and return policies.

Shipping Information

Standard Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $300. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

  • Delivery within 5-7 business days
  • Free for orders over $300
  • $15 for orders under $300
  • Tracking information provided

Express Shipping

Need your items faster? Choose our express shipping option for quicker delivery.

  • Delivery within 2-3 business days
  • $25 flat rate
  • Order by 2pm for same-day dispatch
  • Priority processing

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International delivery times may vary.

  • Delivery within 7-14 business days
  • Shipping costs calculated at checkout
  • Customs and import duties may apply
  • Tracking available for most countries

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy on all full-price items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached.

  • 30-day return window
  • Items must be unworn and unaltered
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Proof of purchase required

Exchange Policy

Prefer a different size or color? We offer free exchanges within 30 days of purchase.

  • Free exchanges within 30 days
  • Subject to availability
  • Original shipping method applies
  • No restocking fees

Non-Returnable Items

Some items are not eligible for return due to hygiene reasons or product nature.

  • Swimwear and intimate apparel
  • Personalized or made-to-order items
  • Final sale items
  • Gift cards

Refund Information

Refund Process

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and process your refund within 5-7 business days.

  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable
  • You will receive email confirmation

Store Credit

If you prefer, we can issue store credit instead of a refund to your original payment method.

  • Issued immediately upon return processing
  • No expiration date
  • Can be used online and in-store
  • Perfect for future purchases

Return Shipping

We offer free return shipping for customers in the contiguous US. International returns are at customer's expense.

  • Free returns for US customers
  • Pre-paid return label included
  • International customers pay return shipping
  • Return within 30 days for full refund

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International shipping times vary by destination but typically take 7-14 business days.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What if my item arrives damaged?

If your item arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund and cover the return shipping costs.

Can I return sale items?

Sale items can be returned within 30 days for store credit or exchange. Final sale items are marked as such and cannot be returned or exchanged.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, log into your account and go to your order history. Select the item you wish to return and follow the instructions. If you checked out as a guest, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

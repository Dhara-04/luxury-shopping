Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Order History' section.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all full-priced items in original condition with tags attached. Final sale items are not eligible for return. For luxury items over $500, we provide a personalized return process with a dedicated concierge.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All international shipments include duties and taxes calculated at checkout for a seamless delivery experience.

How can I request a personal shopper?

Our personal shopping service is available to all clients. You can request a consultation by emailing personalshopper@scartchluxury.com or calling our dedicated personal shopping line at +1 (888) 315-PERSONAL. Our stylists will work with you to understand your preferences and create curated selections.

How do I care for my luxury items?

Each product comes with specific care instructions. For leather goods, we recommend using our luxury care products. For fine jewelry, avoid contact with chemicals and store in provided pouches. We also offer professional cleaning and maintenance services for all items purchased from Scartch Luxury.

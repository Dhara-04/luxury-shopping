We're seeking a creative and detail-oriented Visual Merchandiser to join our retail design team. You'll be responsible for creating visually stunning displays that tell our brand story, enhance the customer experience, and drive sales through strategic product presentation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement compelling visual displays across our retail locations
  • Develop and maintain visual merchandising standards and guidelines
  • Create seasonal window displays that capture brand essence and drive foot traffic
  • Collaborate with marketing and buying teams to align visual strategy with campaigns
  • Train store staff on visual standards and maintenance procedures
  • Analyze sales data to optimize product placement and visual strategies
  • Manage visual merchandising budget and inventory of display materials

Requirements

  • 3+ years of visual merchandising experience in luxury retail or fashion
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating creative visual presentations
  • Knowledge of current visual merchandising trends and techniques
  • Proficiency in SketchUp, Adobe Creative Suite, or other design software
  • Excellent spatial awareness and understanding of store flow
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends during peak seasons
  • Degree in Visual Merchandising, Design, or related field preferred

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and seasonal performance bonuses
  • Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
  • Generous employee discount on products
  • Creative development allowance
  • Opportunities for international travel
  • Collaboration with renowned designers and artists

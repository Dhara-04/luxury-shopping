How Can We Help You?

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our products, services, and policies.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

We offer several shipping options:

- Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (complimentary on orders over $500)
- Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($25 flat rate)
- International Shipping: 5-10 business days (rates vary by destination)

Please note that order processing takes 1-2 business days before shipment.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Order History section.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International orders are shipped via DHL International and typically take 5-10 business days to arrive. Please note that customs duties and import taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations, and these charges are the responsibility of the recipient.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most unused items in their original condition with tags attached. Items must be in their original packaging with proof of purchase. For hygiene reasons, certain items like earrings for pierced ears, swimwear, and intimate apparel cannot be returned. Final sale items are also non-returnable.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return:

1. Log into your account and go to Order History
2. Select the item you wish to return
3. Follow the prompts to generate a return label
4. Package the item securely with all original tags and packaging
5. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Alternatively, you can contact our customer care team for assistance.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your return, it takes 3-5 business days to process. After processing, your bank may take an additional 5-10 business days to post the refund to your account, depending on their policies.

Products & Care

How do I care for my luxury leather goods?

To maintain the beauty of your leather goods:

- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
- Use a soft, dry cloth for regular cleaning
- Avoid contact with water, perfumes, and cosmetics
- Use specialized leather conditioner periodically
- Stuff bags with tissue paper to maintain shape when not in use

Always refer to the care instructions provided with your specific product.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. Scotch Luxury is an authorized retailer of all the brands we carry. We source our products directly from designers and official distributors. Each item comes with its original packaging, authenticity cards, and certificates where applicable. We guarantee the authenticity of every product we sell.

Do you offer repairs or maintenance services?

Yes, we offer repair and maintenance services for items purchased from us. Services include hardware replacement, stitching repairs, leather conditioning, and more. Please contact our customer care team with details about your product and the required service. Note that some services may incur charges depending on the product age and warranty status.

Still Have Questions?

Our customer care team is available to assist you with any additional questions or concerns you may have.

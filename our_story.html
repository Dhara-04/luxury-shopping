Our Story

Discover the journey of Scartch Luxury, where craftsmanship meets elegance and tradition blends with innovation.

The Beginning

Founded in 1998, Scartch Luxury began as a small atelier in Milan, Italy. Our founder, Giovanni Scartch, had a vision to create timeless luxury pieces that would transcend trends and become heirlooms passed down through generations.

With just three master craftsmen and a passion for excellence, we began creating leather goods that quickly gained recognition for their impeccable quality and attention to detail.

Craftsmanship & Quality

At Scartch Luxury, we believe that true luxury lies in the details. Each piece is meticulously crafted by our skilled artisans who have spent decades perfecting their techniques.

We source only the finest materials from around the world - from Italian leathers to Swiss watch movements - ensuring that every product meets our exacting standards of excellence.
